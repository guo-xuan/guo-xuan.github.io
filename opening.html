Ph.D. Research Assistantship

Multiple Ph.D. research assistantship positions are available in the research area of Bioinformatics. We are particularly interested in candidates in the areas of computational genomics, computational proteomics, and integrative omics. Primary application focuses of our research are in but not limited to medical informatics, gene regulatory network, protein identification/quantification, medical records systems, and smart connected health.

The graduate research assistantship includes a monthly stipend and full tuition waiver.

Preferred qualifications:

  • (1) Degree requirement: B.S. or M.S. degree in Computer Science/Engineering, Applied Mathematics, or equivalent
  • (2) Previous research experience with a good publication record

Interested students could send curriculum vitae, along with descriptions of their backgrounds, research interests, and publications (if any), via email, to Prof. Xuan Guo (Xuan.Guo@unt.edu).
